Cut in a relaxed cocoon silhouette from 100% Belgian linen, this dress moves beautifully with the body. Dropped shoulders and wide sleeves create an effortlessly elegant drape — suited for the office or a Cantonments evening dinner.

Featuring a concealed side zip and adjustable inner tie. The linen is pre-washed for softness and only improves with wear.

Composition: 100% Organic Belgian Linen · Lining: 100% TENCEL™ Modal · Length: Midi (approx. mid-calf)

Machine wash at 30°C gentle cycle. Use eco-friendly detergent. Do not tumble dry — lay flat or hang to air dry. Iron on medium heat while slightly damp.

Linen softens with every wash. Natural fibres breathe beautifully and are naturally antibacterial — wear more, wash less.

Sustainability Rating: 4/5 — Excellent

Made from certified organic Belgian linen in a GOTS-certified factory in Portugal. Workers earn above living wage. Packaged in FSC-certified paper, zero single-use plastic. Shipped carbon-neutral. Designed for 200+ wears.
